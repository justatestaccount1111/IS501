My Career in Marketing

A simple overview of where I've been and what's next.

My Projected Career Path is as follows:

  1. Commercial Strategy Analyst
  2. Commercial Strategy Manager
  3. Senior Manager (Strategy or Product Management)
  4. Director
  5. VP

After reaching VP and working at that level for 3-5 years, I will revaluate and either take on a side project, consider a new industry, or go back to school to get a PhD.

To date, I have worked as a:

  • Commercial Strategy MBA Intern
  • Marketing Consultant
  • Product Marketing Senior Associate
  • Ad Buyer
  • Merchandising undergraduate Intern

I can contribute to the business world and the field of marketing in a positive way by engaging in effective but ethical marketing. Many view sales and marketing in a negative light, but I think that at the core, marketing is about understanding customers and helping to meet their needs.
